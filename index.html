My name is Sarthak. I am a mechanical engineering senior at the R.V. College of Engineering, Bengaluru. I am fascinated by robots and have worked on research projects that involve the application of robotics to solve different challenges.

Currently, I am working as an undergraduate researcher at the Theoretical Ecology & Evolution Laboratory at the Center for Ecological Sciences, in the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, under the guidance of Professor Vishwesha Guttal & Jitesh Jhawar. We are building bio-mimetic robotic predatory fishes to study collective behavior and swarming in Serpae tetra. We intend to build upon the observations and incorporate the findings into swarm robots.

I am a visiting undergraduate researcher at the Center for System Design, National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal where I had completed my Summer and Winter Research Internships after my fourth and fifth semesters respectively, under the guidance of Professor KV Gangadharan and Professor Pruthviraj Umesh.

I am also a member of the college robotics club, Astra Robotics, where we build prototype Mars Rovers & drivereless cars. Apart from this, I am an avid quizzer and swimmer as well.
