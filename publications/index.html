Fracture: Computer vision tool to identify fractures and wear.
  • Paper
  • Code
    • Computer-vision tool for identify fractures and wear in mechanical components. A neural network based on Inception v3, is trained on images of the mechanical component, then used to predict the possibility of future wear by inspecting the component image. OpenCV based image-processing unit runs different kernels on the image to identify fractures and surface abrasions.
